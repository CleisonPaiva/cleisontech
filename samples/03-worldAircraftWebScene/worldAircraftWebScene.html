About This Project

This project presents a 3D interactive globe using Esri’s WebScene technology to showcase geographic data in a dynamic and immersive way. The scene is centered around Vitória, Brazil, and uses lighting effects to simulate realistic shadows and daylight conditions.

Explore the visualization by zooming, panning, and rotating the globe to analyze spatial relationships and landscape perspectives in a real-world 3D context.

Technologies: ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript (SceneView & WebScene), HTML, CSS, Esri Widgets (Zoom), 3D Lighting & Camera Positioning.
